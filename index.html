    Join Us

    Founded by FGLI students, we know personally the hindrances that many FGLI students encounter. Often, fearing judgment, students try to hide away this part of their identity. They become reluctant to reach out for help about things that, in their peers’ perspectives, they should already know about. As a result, this causes FGLI students to always be several steps behind their fellow peers in the educational journey. 

    ​

    Being FGLI shouldn’t be the reason for a student to hold back on their own potential or from pursuing their aspirations.

    ​

    Join us in our mission of empowering FGLI youth.
